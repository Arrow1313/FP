%einleitung zu dem experiment.
%auf die einstellungen, die vor dem versuch gemacht werden, eingehen, oder auf eine anleitung dazu verweisen.

%hinter der einleitung kann der allgemeine theoretische hintergrund in einer zusätzlichen section erklärt werden
The aim of the experiment is to analyse the inversionspectrum of ammoniak by using microwave radiation. The spectrallines resulting from the inversion potential of ammoniak can be explained in a quantummechanical way. In this experiment, we want to identify the absorbtion lines of amoniak, which are predicted from the quantum mechanical theory. We also want to calculate the quadrupol constant of ammoniak from the hyperfinsturcture split-up. At the end we want to measure the pressure dependence of the line-width (FWHM) of the absorption lines, because this is the biggest fraction of the line widening effects at this frequency.
